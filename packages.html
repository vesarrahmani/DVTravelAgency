Maldive

The Maldives is famous for a number of reasons, including its vibrant culture, pristine beaches, sprawling greenery, and fascinating landmarks. Apart from the numerous enchanting landmarks, Maldives is also famous for water sports and other adventure activities.

New York

New York, also known as the Big Apple, is a city which offers infinite things to do and places to see. The city of skyscrapers has starred in films and novels that have made it a dream destination for many. A trip to New York is a unique experience, what are you waiting for?

Bali

The mountains of Bali (also easily accessible from Ubud) are a part of the island definitely worth visiting. Crowned with Bali's famous beautiful rice terraces, the mountains are dotted with several waterfalls (mentioned above) as well as towering volcanoes.

Dubai

Dubai is one of the most popular tourist destinations on the planet. The place is renowned for its opulent shopping, luxury hotels and resorts, vibrant nightlife, awe-inspiring architecture and awesome beaches.
